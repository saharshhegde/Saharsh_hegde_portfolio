About Me

I'm a passionate cybersecurity enthusiast and ethical hacker, currently preparing for the CEH certification. Over time, I’ve mastered tools like Burp Suite, Metasploit, Nmap, Nessus, Wireshark, SQLMap and Aircrack-ng, while completing simulated environments on Hack The Box and TryHackMe.

I’ve built live security projects including:

  • 🕵️‍♂️ Malware Analysis Sandbox (MITRE ATT&CK based)
